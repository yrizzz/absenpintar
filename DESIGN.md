# 🎨 Design System - AbsenPintar (Frosted Midnight Slate & Cobalt Glass)

## Design Philosophy

### Core Principles
1. **High-End Bespoke Aesthetic** - Completely custom, high-contrast, premium dark slate workspace. Bypasses typical AI-generated template aesthetics by using luxurious midnight glass textures.
2. **Deep Midnight Canvas** - A beautiful, non-fatiguing, sophisticated deep navy-slate canvas (`#090e1a` / `#0f172a`) styled with glowing ambient orbital rings.
3. **Luminous Cobalt & Emerald Accents** - Primary touchpoints use premium cobalt-blue gradients flowing to bright sky and emerald tips, matching the advanced verified telemetry indicators.
4. **Frosted Glass Panel Architecture** - Semi-translucent panels with fine white/10 border contours and rich background blurs (`backdrop-blur-xl`) that feel solid, sleek, and highly tactile.
5. **Fluid Micro-Animations** - Dynamic float parameters, pulsing biometric elements, and glowing focus border scales.

- **Primary Actions**: `bg-gradient-to-r from-blue-600 via-sky-500 to-emerald-400`

### Text Color Hierarchy
- **Luminous Headers**: `#ffffff` (High-contrast Outfit geometric fonts)
- **Subtle Descriptions**: `#94a3b8` (Muted Slate)
